About Earth & Ember

Hi, I’m Jill! Welcome to Earth & Ember, where I craft handmade soaps inspired by nature and passion. As part of a family-owned business with deep roots in the soaping industry, I’ve grown up surrounded by the craft of formulation and production. What began as a small venture has evolved into a full-scale operation, with our own factory based in Manila and Bulacan, where we create a wide range of products—from detergents and fabric softeners to powdered soaps and premium fragrances.

My passion for quality, natural ingredients led me to focus on organic soaps—blends made with intention, free from harsh chemicals, and designed to nourish both skin and spirit. Whether it’s a soothing lavender bar or a deeply moisturizing shea butter blend, I believe that bathing should be more than routine—it should be a moment of care and connection.

At Earth & Ember, I’m proud to carry forward our family legacy while also exploring new ways to bring clean, conscious, and luxurious self-care to those who value the power of nature.
